2. Product / Service Description

LedgerSeiri is a cloud-based software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform designed to help cross-border e-commerce sellers organize and analyze accounting-related data such as sales, platform fees, and expenses.

The service focuses on data organization and provides visualization and export functionality (CSV / Excel).

3. Pricing & Billing
